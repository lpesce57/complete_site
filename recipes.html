Crunchwrap Supreme

Origin: Taco Bell
Source: Taco Bell Website
Category: Comfort Dish

There's an undeniable satisfaction in indulging in a Crunchwrap Supreme whenever hunger strikes. Fusing a traditional Mexican taco with a generously sized flattened burrito creates a symphony of flavors that resonates perfectly with my taste buds.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Ground Beef
  • Circular Taco Shell
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Melted Cheese
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Taco Bell Seasoning
  • Cheese Sauce

Recipe Steps

  1. Make the ground beef with taco seasoning, season it well and use Taco Bell spices from Target.
  2. Lay the tortilla on the grill and put cheese sauce on top of it, smooth it out in a circular motion.
  3. Next, put the circular crunch taco shell on top of it while adding a generous amount of sour cream on it, don't be shy!
  4. Add your seasoning to the ground beef onto the tortilla.
  5. Finally, add your diced tomatoes, lettuce, and cheese.
  6. Wrap your Crunchwrap pentagon style and then grill it on a hot pan for about 4 minutes on medium heat, both sides.
  7. Take it off the pan and then cut it in half and you have your Crunchwrap supreme.

Double Chocolate Cookies

Origin: Michigan
Source: Family Recipe
Category: Dessert

My daughter learned to make these cookies at a baking camp at Zingermanns and has tweaked the recipe to fit the taste buds of her siblings. They are extremely sugary so the salt helps to balance it. Note, these cookies are best eaten very quickly.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Packed light or dark brown sugar
  • Large egg
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chunks (melted)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chunks

Recipe Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  2. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the bowl
  3. Beat the egg well
  4. Melt your chocolate in the microwave for approximatley 30 seconds
  5. Add the melted chocolate to the bowl with the rest of your ingredients
  6. In a separate bowl combine the flour, baking soda, cocoa powder and salt
  7. Combine both bowls in order to combine the wet and dry ingredients
  8. Add the unmelted chocolate chunks.
  9. Form about 15 cookies and place on a baking sheet. Make sure the cookies are adequatley spaced between each other.
  10. Cook for 12 to 13 minutes at 350 degrees.

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Origin: America
Source: Alex Likens
Category: Main Dish

This is a dish that is great at all times of year. It is a classic dish and is often considered a comfort dish. It is great for any situation whether you are on the go, in a rush, or just looking for a tasty meal in your home.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Recipe Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread and place them on a plate
  2. Put a thick layer of jelly on one piece of the bread
  3. Put a thick layer of peanut butter on the other piece of the bread
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together so they face each other
